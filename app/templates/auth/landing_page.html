More to Discover

COPA is a SaaS platform that enables full tracked management of all the resources of cooperatives.

Membership management.

This is the membership management and communication tool that was designed to impacted hundreds of thousands of people from cooperatives socities. Better organize your members, improve your communication, and empower members to connect together, communicate online, and collaborate towards higher goals. COPA's membership management module today can be used by all cooperatives regardless their economic sectors.

Stock management.

Stock management feature is suitable for cooperatives (small, medium to large) in retail, wholesale, manufacturing, food production sectors which have requirements to manage stock / inventory levels with members engagement. Module covers the entire business cycle in stock / inventory management. Communication, Payment and other functions are integrated in the module for more than just managing inventories. COPA's Inventory management provides features that give cooperatives and members information they need to manage costs and maximize profitability.

Reporting

Comprehensive and Automatic Reporting is a COPA platform's module that provides cooperative behavior analytics, making it easy for cooperatives and their stakeholders of all types to optimize business processes. We equip cooperatives, members and their stakeholders with the resources needed to have a bigger picture of what's going on in the organization, shifting management from reactive to proactive. By capturing unbiased data, COPA gives cooperative and their members the power to understand how work gets done, what affects productivity, and how to streamline processes to create an efficient business.
