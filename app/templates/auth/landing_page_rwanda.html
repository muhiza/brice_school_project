Ikoranabuhanga mu Makoperative.

COPA ni porogaramu ya mudasobwa igenewe ama Koperative mu rwego rwo kuyafasha koroshya imicungira yayo ya buri munsi, irimo; gucuma abanyamuryango, icunga mutungo, itumanaho, ububiko, gutegura ama raporo, igena migambi n'ibindi bitandukanye.

Menya ibindi Iyandikishe

Murakaza neza.

Sobanukirwa birambuye.

Ikoranabuhanga rihuriza hamwe ibikenewe byose mu gucunga Koperative z'ubwoko butandukanye, aho gukemura ikibazo kimwe mu bindi.

Gusubiza.

COPA ishobora gukora neza ku bikoresho bitandukanye, yaba telephone cg mudasobwa.

Guhindura

Ushobora guhindura porogaramu ya COPA mu buryo ushaka ndetse ukagenda uhitamo ibyo ushaka kubone igihe ushakiye.

Ibikoresho

Hari ibikoresha bitandukanye byagenewe gukemura ibibazo byihariye, mu micungire ya buri munsi ya Koperative.

Gusangira amakuru.

Porogaramu ya COPA, ifasha gusanginza amakuru abafatanyabikorwa bose ba Koperative mu buryo bwihuse.

Imfashanyigisho

Porogaramu ya COPA, izana n'imfasha nyigisho ziteguye neza zifasha abayikoresha gusobanukirwa buri kimwe n'icyo gikora.

Ubufasha

Mu gihe ukoresha COPA, ugezwaho ubufasha buhoraho (24/7) ndetse ugahabwa n'ibiba bigezweho.

Ibirimo

Bimwe mu bigize COPA.

COPA ni porogaramu itangwa nka serivise, ifasha Koperative gucunga ibikorwa byayo byose bya buri munsi.

Gucunga Abanyamuryango

Uru ni uruhande rwo gucunga abanyamuryango ba Cooperative no kubika amakuru yabo hakoreshejwe system. Uburyo bwo gukora amatsinda / groups agizwe n’abanyamuryango muri cooperative. Urugero: Amatsinda yo kuzigama no kugurizanya. Uburyo bwo gushyira abanyamuryango mu ma zones / amahangari babarizwamo, no kugenzura uko umusaruro ugemurwa hagendewe ku ma zones / amahangari.

Gucunga ububiko.

Gucunga no kubika amakuru y'ububiko bwa Koperative mu buryo buhoraho no gutanga ama report yerekeye ibyakozwe mu gihe runaka. Urugero:Ububiko, Imisaruro, Inyongera musaruro, Imisanzu, Ibirarane, Ibihano n'Ibindi.

Gutegura raporo

Gutegura ubwoko bwa raporo zitandukanye no kuzisangiza abandi bafatanya bikorwa bose ba Koperative n’izindi nzego zikorana na Koperative. Urugero:Federation, Unions, Inganda, Banks / Sacco, abafatanyabikorwa ndetse n’abandi bagenwe na Koperative.
