COPA, is for cooperative management.

Automate & Integrate your cooperative's daily activities to improve member's socio-economic situations by encouraging the benefits of co-operative enterprises using technology.

Learn more





Membership management

COPA allows one to register all members on the system where they access to their cooperative records online.

Stock management

Manage and track the daily movements and changes within the cooperative’s stock.

Financial
management

COPA acts as an online treasurer. All one does is record financials and COPA reconciles the records.

Reporting

COPA generates the following reports; Member Statements, Cashflow, Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss.

Blockchain-powered
e-wallet

Enjoy the power of automatic reconciliations by transacting using Mobile money.

Traceability

Supply chain traceability: in many supply chains, a blockchain can assist in providing an immutable record from the provenance to the retail store of a product.
