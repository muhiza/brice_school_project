COPA, Is for Cooperatives.

An all-in-one solution for digitizing all operations of business cooperatives to help better planning, evaluation of day-to-day activities and efficiently monitoring their financial situation. COPA can also be used by Unions, Federations as well as the Rwanda Cooperative Agency to access different data for planning and budgeting purposes.

Features

  • Supply chain management

    With COPA, cooperatives tracks the management of the flow of goods and services, involves the movement and storage of raw materials, of work-in-process inventory, and of finished goods as well as end to end order fulfillment from point of origin to point of consumption.

  • Membersip management

    COPA is everything you'll ever need to manage and grow a cooperative. A user-friendly alternative to the spreadsheet-like software you'll find elsewhere. Membership management, recurring billing, Email and text messaging, session booking, attendance tracking and much more.

  • Payments

    With COPA, by utilizing blockchain technology, we have created an advanced financial platform built into a user-friendly mobile Banking app, in which members can manage their savings, payments and investments in an honest, transparent and secure way. Through COPA, cash societies can be eliminated through the adoption of our Mobile Banking App, which is operating with full reserve in a bank account. COPA offers a variety of services cost free, simple and instant.

  • Credit scoring.

    COPA provide a statistical analysis performed for lenders and financial institutions to access a cooperative's members creditworthiness based on their stock movements. The data can be used by lenders to help decide on whether to extend or deny credit.

  • Reporting Tools

    Powered by powerful, secure and efficient BlockChain technology, COPA provide different reports from diverse data that's collected from different cooperatives' economic activities accross the country. Different institutions can access different data for planning, budgeting purposes and other activities.
