Ibibazo n’ibisubizo...

Ni ayahe ma Koperative yakoresha porogaramu ya COPA?

Porogaramu ya COPA yakorewe ama koperative ari mu bwoko bwose bw’ubukungu, ariko mu ntangiriro ikaba yaratangiye yibanda ku ma Koperative y’ubuhinzi, mu gihe n’izindi Koperative zishobora kuba zikoresha COPA ntakibazo, ntago zizejwe gusangamo ibyo zikeneye byose.

Ese amahuriro, impuzamahuriro ndetse n’urugaga bishobora nabyo gukoresha COPA?

Yego, imvano, isesengurwa ndetse n’ishyirwa mu bikorwa rya porogaramu ya COPA byose bishingiye ku mahame shingiro agenga ama Koperative ndetse n’igishushanyo cya’ama Koperative ku rwego rw’igihunga na mpuzamahanga.

Ni gute abanyamuryango b’ama Koperative bashobora kungukira mu gukoresha COPA?

Nk’uko Koperative iba ari iyabanyamuryango, ni nabo dushyira imbere mubyo dukora. Abanyamuryango bashobora kubona amakuru ya buri munsi ari muri Koperative abareba, yaba ayerekeye imigabane yabo, umutungo wa Koperative, isoko, ibihingwa, ihembwa ndetse n’ama matangazo aba abareba igihe icyo aricyo cyose. Begerezwe serivisi z’imari zirimo nko gukoresha amakuru yabo bakaba babona inguzanyo mu bigo by’imari ndetse no kohererezanya amafara haba hagati yabo ubwobo cg Koperative.

Ikoranabuhanga rya mushyinguranyi na COPA bihuriye he?

Ubu ni uburyo bucungiwe umutekano cyane, bwifashishwa mu kohererezanya amafaranga hagati y’abantu ndetse No kubika amakuru mu buryo bwizewe kandi abantu bayafiteho uburenganzira bungana.

COPA yifashisha ikoranabuhanga rya mushyinguranyi mu gukurikirana uruhererekana cg Supply chain traceability, Ni uburyo COPA ifasha mu gukurikirana inzira igihingwa cg igicuruzwa kinyuramo, kuva kiri mu murima Kugeza kigeze kw’isoko, ibi bizana n’inyungu zitandukanye zirimo nko kwihutisha akazi, kubika amakuru y’ingenzi mu buryo bwizewe ndetse no kongera gukorera mu mucyo mu gucunga Koperative.

Ni gute abantu badafite ubumenyi bw’ibanze kubijyanye n’ikoranabuhanga bafashwa gukoresha COPA?

Dutanga amahugurwa afasha abakoresha porogaramu ya COPA kugira ubumenyi bw’ibinze kw’ikoranabuhanga rikenerwa mu buzima bwa buri munsi kugirango abantu babona serivisi zitandukanye.
