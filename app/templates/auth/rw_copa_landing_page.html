COPA, Uburyo bworoshye bwo gucunga ibikorwa bya Koperative.

Porogaramu yambere y’ikoranahanga yakorewe gucunga ibikorwa byose bya Koperative, guhuza inzego zose zikorana n’ama Koperative mu buryo bwo gusangira amakuru ndetse no kongera uruhare rw’abanyamuryango mu bikorwa bya Koperative bya buri munsi.

REBA IBINDI





Gucunga abanyamuryango.

Uburyo bwo kwandika imyirondoro y’abanyamuryango ndetse no kubimerera gukurikirana ibikorwa bya Koperative bya buri munsi hafashishijwe Telephone zabo zigendanwa.

Gucunga ububiko (Stock).

Mu bikorwa bya buri munsi bya Koperative hari ibyo Abanyamuryango baba bazana cg bavana mu Bubiko bwa Koperative. COPA ikourikirana ibyo Bikorwa ndetse ikanategura raporo za buri munsi Zerekeye ububiko.

Icunga mutungo.

COPA, ni nk’umucunga mutungo wa Koperative uri kuri murandasi Mu gihe amakuru yerekeye imari muri Koperative yatanzwe, Porogaramu igufasha gutegura ama raporo yose y’icunga Mutungo aba akenewe.

Gutegura ama raporo.

Nk’uko ama Koperative aba afite abafatanya bikorwa benshi Bose niko baba bashaka raporo z’imbitse zijyanye N’ibikorwa bya Koperative mu gihe runaka, COPA ibyo ibikora neza.

Mushyinguranyi.

Ubu ni uburyo bucungiwe umutekano cyane, bwifashishwa mu Kohererezanya amafaranga hagati y’abantu ndetse No kubika amakuru mu buryo bwizewe kandi Abantu bayafiteho uburenganzira bungana.

Gukurikirana uruhererekane.

Gukurikirana uruhererekana cg Supply chain traceability Ni uburyo COPA ifasha mu gukurikirana inzira igihingwa Cg igicuruzwa kinyuramo, kuva kiri mu murima Kugeza kigeze kw’isoko, ibi bizana n’inyungu zitandukanye Zirimo nko kwihutisha akazi no kubika amakuru y’ingenzi.

Hitamo igiciro kijyanye n’ibyo ukeneye.

COPA yadufashije cyane kwihutisha akazi Ndetse no gufasha abakozi gukora inshingano Zabo za buri munsi neza. Ubu dushobora gukusanya Amakuru neza ndetse tukanayabika mu buryo Bwizewe.

Olive Nirere.

Ubu ntago tugikoresha igihe kerekira ndetse n’amafaranga Mu ngendo za buri munsi dutwaye ama raporo mu ntoki No ku mpapuro ku bafatanya bikorwa bacu nka mbere. Ubu COPA niyo ibikora mu buryo bwihuse.
