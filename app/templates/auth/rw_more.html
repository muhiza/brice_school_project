COPA, Uburyo bworoshye bwo gucunga ibikorwa bya Koperative.


Imicungire mibi y'amakoperative ni ikibanzo gikunze kugarukwaho cyane, haba mu baturage ndetse no mw'itangazamakuru, aho usanga ubujura bukomeza kugenda bwiyongera, ubwumvikane buke hagati y'abanyamuryango ndetse n'abayoboze b'amakoperative, ahanini ibi bikaba biterwa n'imicungire y'ayo ma koperative igikorwa mu buryo butajyanye n'igihe, hakaba hakoreshwa impapuru ndetse n'ubundi buryo burushaho kongera imicungire mibi ndetse n'ubujura. Ni mu gihe kandi ama koperative afatiye runini abaturage (abanyamuryango) baba bayabarizwamo ndetse n'iterambere ry'igihugu muri rusange.

Ni muri urwo rwego ikompanyi Extra Technologies, ikorera mu Rwanda, ikaba isanzwe ikora program za mudasobwa zigezweho, yakoze program zihariye mu micungire y'ama koperative mu buryo bugezweho hifashishijwe ikoranabuhanga, aho bimwe mu bibazo nk'ibijyanye n'icunga mutungo, itumanaho hagati ya koperative n'abanyamuryango, igena migambi, gutegura ama raporo ndetse no kuyasangiza abafatanyabikorwa, ibigo by’imari n'abandi baba bakorana na koperative, gucunga abanyamuryango n'imigabane yabo ndetse n'uko igenda yiyongera cg ikagabanuka bakajya babimenyeshwa buri kwezi bitewe n'uko koperative igenda itera imbere cyangwa igasubira inyuma mu mikorere yayo ya buri munsi.

Izi program zose zitangirwa ubuntu ku ma koperative yose zikaba ziboneka hifashishijwe murandasi (Internet), telephone zigendanwa, zaba izigezweho ndetse n'izindi zisanzwe. Zikaba zitezweho gutanga umusanzu mu kongera uruhare ry'abanyamuryango mu micungire y'amakoperative yabo haba mu kuyateza imbere ndetse no gutera imbere kw'abanyamurango by’umwihariko.

MUHIZA Frank uyobora Extra technologies agira ati "Ntawakwirengagiza uruhare rw'amakoperative mw'iterambere ry'igihugu kuko ariyo atanga akazi mu banyagihugu benshi, kubaka ibikorwa remezo bitandukanye ndetse n'ubukungu bw'igihugu ahanini bukaba bushingiye kuma koperative aboneka mu bice bitandukanye by'imibireho y'abaturage, nko mubuhinzi, gutwara abantu n'ibintu (transport), ubucukuzi bw'amabuye y'agaciro, ubucuruzi, serivise n'ibindi. Ibyo rero bigaragaza ko imicungire yayo igomba kwitabwaho mu buryo bwihariye"

Akomeze agira ati "Ikibazo cy'imicungire y'ama koperative ni ikibazo gikomeye, gisaba ubufatanye bwa leta ndetse n'abikorera mu buryo bwihariye ariko twizeye ko kwinjiza ikoranabuhanga mu micungire yayo ari intangiriro nziza mu gukemura ibibazo biyagaragaramo"

Avuga kandi ko hakiri imbogamizi ahanini zishingiye ku bumenyi abaturage bafite ku ikoranabuhanga bukiri hasi cyane muri rusange (ICT illiteracy).

Ati "Nkubu turi gutanga program dufite zose ku buntu ariko ikibazo kijyanye n'imyumvire ndetse n'ubumenyi bucye mu baturage kubijyanye n'ikoranabuhanga buracyari imbogamizi ikomeye cyane, aha urebye niho hakenewe ubufatanye hagati y’inzego zitandukanye mu guhugura abaturage ku bumenyi bw'ibanze ku bijyanye n'ikoranabuhanga, tukaba dusaba ibigo bya leta bifite aho bihuriye n'amakoperative kudufasha gusakaza izi program ndetse no gutanga amahugurwa, cyane ko dufite gahunda yo guhugura ama koperative abyifuza, ibijyanye n'ikoranabuhanga rigezweho ndetso no kurikoresha mu mirimo ya buri munsi yayo." Raporo yo muri 2018 y’ikigo gishinzwe guteza imbere ama koperative mu Rwanda, RCA, igaragaza ko mu Rwanda hari koperative 8,995 zikora imirimo itandukanye, zibumbiyemo abaturage ku 3,816,591 bagera ku 297,996, abagabo akaba ari 179,510 (60%) naho abagore akaba ari 118,486 (40%) zikaba zimaze kugera ku mugaba shingiro ungana na 4,878,087,148 Frw.
