AICOS is a SaaS platform that enables full tracked management of all the resources of cooperatives.

Features...

Membership Managment

AICOS allows cooperative to register all members on the system where they access to their records online.

Stock Management

AICOS come with a set of tools for tracking inventory levels, orders, sales and deliveries in Cooperative.

Automatic Reporting

Using AICOS, the users only needs to enter data as they come and AICOS handles the reporting behalf of the users Automatically.

Expense Management

AICOS allows cooperatives to track expenses as they occur, be it for land purchases or payments for services.

Loan Management

For cooperatives that do internal lending, AICOS provides a platform for recording these loans.

Penalty Management

Cooperative can penalize members for issues such a late coming and this will be reflected on the members' statement

Available online & Fully responsive

AICOS is available online, 24/7 with a support team to assist the Cooperatives' staff and groups in utilizing the software

Easy to Setup, Use & Customize

AICOS requires only five steps to set up the platform and start using the software; Sign up, Register Members, Add Bank Accounts, Setup Contribution and Back Date Group Finances

Available on Mobile
